Forte Labs Community

Welcome to the home of the Forte Labs Community!

You belong here.

All members of the Forte Labs community are invited to join the Forte Labs Slack. To join, take the following steps:

  1. Read our Code of Conduct.
  2. Sign up for our Slack with your email by clicking here.
  3. Add a photo to your account, so we can recognize you across different platforms.
  4. Have a look at the channels + join a few - or even start your own!
  5. If you have a smartphone, you may want to download the Slack app and access the community there.

We are currently using the free Slack tier. On that tier, archived messages after the last 10k messages will not be visible - be sure to save any messages that you see or write that are important or interesting to you! The Evernote Slack integration is available to help you take, save, and share notes, using the /note, /clip, and /find commands respectively.
